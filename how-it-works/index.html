How We Work

Two streamlined paths to get you the right seals, fast.

Our Process

Two Paths, One Goal

Whether you need a standard catalog seal or a fully custom engineered solution, we have a streamlined process to deliver results.

Standard Seals

Fast turnaround for catalog parts

1

Customer Inquiry

You reach out with your seal requirements via email or contact form.

2

Spec Confirmation

We confirm model, size, material, and key dimensions with you.

3

Stock Check

We verify inventory availability across our warehouse network.

4

Cost & Lead Time Verification

We confirm pricing and exact delivery timelines.

5

Price Quotation

You receive a detailed formal quote within 12 hours.

6

Order Confirmation

You confirm the order and we process it immediately.

7

Production

In-stock items ship fast; out-of-stock items go to procurement.

8

Delivery

Logistics arrangement confirmed — your seals are on the way.

Custom Engineered Seals

From drawing or sample to production-ready

1

Customer Inquiry

Tell us about your application and what you need.

2

Drawing or Sample Received

We receive your technical drawing or physical sample.

3

Metric Conversion & Engineering Drawing

We convert units and produce detailed engineering drawings.

4

Supplier Quotation & Lead Time

Our manufacturing partners provide cost and timeline estimates.

5

Quotation to Customer

You receive a comprehensive quote with all specifications.

6

Order Confirmation

You approve and we kick off the manufacturing process.

7

Component Sampling

Many go straight to batch after drawing approval; sampling when needed.

8

Full Component Readiness & Production

All components are sourced and production begins.

9

Sample Optimization

Tolerance refinement and performance fine-tuning.

10

Batch Production & Delivery

Full-scale manufacturing and shipment to your door.

Not sure which path fits your needs?

Tell us about your application, and we'll guide you from there.
