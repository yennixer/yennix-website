Industry Challenges

Common Sealing Pain Points

Marine & Shipbuilding operations face unique sealing challenges driven by process fluids, operating conditions, and equipment environments. Understanding these challenges is the first step to finding the right seal solution.

  • Severe corrosion from saltwater and marine environments
  • Biofouling and marine growth on seal components
  • Vibration and shock loads from shipboard operation
  • Limited maintenance access during voyages
  • Classification society and IMO requirements

Our Solutions

YENNIX Marine & Shipbuilding Solutions

YENNIX marine-grade mechanical seals are engineered to withstand the corrosive challenge of seawater and the demanding conditions of shipboard operation. From ballast systems to engine cooling pumps, our marine seals deliver reliable performance with corrosion-resistant materials and robust construction — reducing maintenance at sea and in port.

Seawater Corrosion Resistance

316L stainless steel, duplex stainless, and Hastelloy metal components with EPDM or FKM elastomers resist saltwater corrosion.

Abrasion & Erosion Resistance

Silicon carbide seal faces handle sediment and particulate-laden seawater commonly found in ballast and cooling systems.

Vibration-Tolerant Design

Robust drive mechanisms and flexible mounting accommodate the vibration and misalignment typical of marine pump installations.

Ballast & Cooling Specialists

Proven solutions for ballast pumps, seawater cooling systems, bilge pumps, and other critical shipboard services.
