Industry Challenges

Common Sealing Pain Points

Chemical Processing operations face unique sealing challenges driven by process fluids, operating conditions, and equipment environments. Understanding these challenges is the first step to finding the right seal solution.

  • Chemical attack on seal faces and elastomers
  • Leakage of hazardous or toxic process fluids
  • Rapid wear in corrosive and high-temperature chemical service
  • Long lead times for specialty chemical seal materials
  • Safety compliance and emissions regulations

YENNIX Chemical Processing Solutions

YENNIX chemical processing seals are engineered to handle the most aggressive media — from strong acids and caustics to organic solvents and high-temperature process fluids. With a comprehensive range of material combinations including SiC, TC, Hastelloy, and FFKM, we provide reliable sealing solutions that protect personnel, equipment, and the environment.

Broad Material Portfolio

Silicon carbide, tungsten carbide, carbon graphite seal faces paired with Viton, EPDM, NBR, or FFKM elastomers — matched to your exact chemistry.

Double Seal Options

M74D and MT560D double seals provide zero-leakage containment for toxic, carcinogenic, or high-value process fluids.

Hastelloy & Specialty Metals
