Industry Challenges

Common Sealing Pain Points

Food & Beverage operations face unique sealing challenges driven by process fluids, operating conditions, and equipment environments. Understanding these challenges is the first step to finding the right seal solution.

  • Food safety and FDA compliance requirements
  • Hygienic design with no crevices for bacterial growth
  • CIP (Clean-in-Place) and SIP (Sterilization-in-Place) compatibility
  • Seal degradation from caustic cleaners and sanitizers
  • Wide range of operating temperatures in cooking and cooling processes

Our Solutions

YENNIX Food & Beverage Solutions

YENNIX food-grade mechanical seals are designed to meet the strict hygiene and safety requirements of the food and beverage industry. With FDA-compliant materials, hygienic surface finishes, and designs that withstand CIP and SIP cycles, our seals help food processors maintain product safety while maximizing equipment uptime.

FDA-Compliant Materials

Food-grade EPDM and Viton elastomers, silicon carbide seal faces, and 316L stainless steel components all meeting FDA food-contact standards.

Hygienic Design

Smooth, polished surfaces and crevice-free construction prevent product buildup and bacterial harborage.

CIP/SIP Compatible

Robust seal designs withstand the thermal and chemical stress of repeated clean-in-place and sterilization cycles.

Wide Temperature Range

From cold product filling to hot processing — our seals perform reliably across the full food production temperature spectrum.
