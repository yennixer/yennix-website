Industry Challenges

Common Sealing Pain Points

Water Treatment operations face unique sealing challenges driven by process fluids, operating conditions, and equipment environments. Understanding these challenges is the first step to finding the right seal solution.

  • Corrosion from chlorinated and chemically treated water
  • Seal failure in abrasive slurry and sediment-laden water
  • Downtime costs in continuous-operation treatment plants
  • Difficulty sourcing replacement seals for older pump fleets
  • Compliance with drinking water and environmental regulations

Our Solutions

YENNIX Water Treatment Solutions

YENNIX delivers proven sealing solutions engineered specifically for water treatment environments. Our seals handle everything from clean water circulation to aggressive chemical dosing and heavy sludge processing — with corrosion-resistant materials and designs optimized for long service life.

Corrosion-Resistant Materials

Silicon carbide seal faces, 316SS metal parts, and EPDM/Viton elastomers resist chlorine, ozone, and water treatment chemicals.

Abrasion-Tolerant Designs

Tungsten carbide faces and robust spring mechanisms handle sediment, grit, and slurry in raw water and wastewater streams.

Energy-Efficient Operation

Precision-lapped seal faces minimize friction and power consumption — critical for high-volume pump fleets.
