Industry Challenges

Common Sealing Pain Points

Mining & Mineral Processing operations face unique sealing challenges driven by process fluids, operating conditions, and equipment environments. Understanding these challenges is the first step to finding the right seal solution.

  • Severe abrasive wear from slurry and mineral particles
  • Shaft deflection and misalignment in heavy-duty pumps
  • Frequent seal replacement increasing operational costs
  • Dewatering pump reliability in underground operations
  • Harsh operating environments with limited maintenance access

Our Solutions

YENNIX Mining & Mineral Processing Solutions

YENNIX heavy-duty seals are built to withstand the brutal conditions of mining and mineral processing. From slurry handling to dewatering pumps, our seals feature abrasion-resistant materials and robust designs that deliver extended service life in the toughest operating environments — reducing downtime and lowering total cost of ownership.

Abrasion-Resistant Faces

Tungsten carbide and silicon carbide seal faces resist the erosive wear of slurry, tailings, and mineral-laden process water.

Heavy-Duty Construction

Reinforced drive mechanisms and robust spring designs handle shaft runout, vibration, and thermal cycling common in mining operations.

Dewatering Seal Specialists

Custom Flygt-compatible and submersible pump seals for underground and open-pit dewatering applications.

Fast Delivery to Remote Sites

Extensive inventory of mining-sector seal models with expedited shipping to keep operations running.
