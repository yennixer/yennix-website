Who We Are

Your Agile Seal Supply Partner

YENNIX is a professional mechanical seal supplier dedicated to serving industrial clients worldwide. We bridge the gap between premium quality and accessibility — delivering precision-engineered seals at competitive prices with unmatched responsiveness.

Unlike large manufacturers with rigid MOQs and long lead times, we offer a flexible, responsive approach — whether you need 1 seal or 10,000.

We serve maintenance companies, equipment distributors, plant engineers, and procurement teams who value speed, reliability, and expert guidance. Our team of sealing specialists is always ready to help you find the right solution for your application.

Our Journey

A Decade of Sealing Excellence

From a small workshop to a global mechanical seal supplier, YENNIX has grown through continuous innovation and unwavering commitment to quality.

2015
Company Founded
YENNIX was established with a vision to deliver high-quality mechanical seals at competitive prices. Started with 5 engineers and a 2,000m² production facility.
2018
Factory Expansion
Expanded production facility to 8,000m² with new CNC machining centers and a dedicated cleanroom assembly area. Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification.

Global Expansion
